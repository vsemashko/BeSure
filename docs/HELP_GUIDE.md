## Creating Questions

### How to Create a Question

1. Tap the **+ (Create)** button in the tab bar
2. Enter your question (e.g., "Which outfit should I wear to the party?")
3. Add 2-6 options (text or images)
4. Set an expiration time (5 min to 7 days)
5. Choose privacy settings (public, friends-only)
6. Review point cost
7. Tap "Post Question"

### Question Options

**Text Options:**
- Up to 200 characters per option
- Clear, concise descriptions work best
- Example: "Red dress with heels" vs "Option A"

**Image Options:**
- Tap the image icon to add photos
- Take a new photo or choose from gallery
- Recommended: Square images, 1:1 ratio
- Maximum size: 10 MB per image

### Expiration Times

Choose how long your question stays active:
- **5-15 minutes:** Super urgent decisions
- **1-6 hours:** Same-day decisions
- **1 day:** Default for most questions
- **3 days:** Complex or non-urgent decisions
- **7 days:** Long-term planning questions

**Tip:** Urgent questions cost fewer points but get fewer votes

### Privacy Settings

**Public (Default):**
- Visible to all BeSure users
- Appears in public feeds
- Can be shared outside the app
- Best for getting maximum votes

**Friends Only:**
- Visible only to users you follow who follow you back
- More personal and private
- Fewer votes but more trusted opinions
- Great for personal decisions

**Anonymous:**
- Your username is hidden
- Question appears without attribution
- Your profile doesn't show the question
- Use for sensitive or personal topics

### Point Costs

Question costs vary based on:
- **Base cost:** 10 points
- **Image uploads:** +2 points per image
- **Urgency:** Longer durations cost more
- **Visibility:** Public costs more than friends-only

**Examples:**
- Simple text question (1 day): 10 points
- Question with 4 images (1 day): 18 points
- Urgent question (1 hour): 8 points

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is BeSure free to use?**
A: Yes! BeSure is completely free. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but core functionality will always be free.

**Q: How do I get more points?**
A: Vote on other users' questions. Each vote earns you +2 points. Maintain daily streaks and complete challenges for bonus points.

**Q: Why do I need points to create questions?**
A: The point system ensures fair participation. It encourages everyone to help others before asking for help, creating a balanced, engaged community.

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Yes, you can change your username in Settings ‚Üí Edit Profile. However, frequent changes may be limited to prevent abuse.

**Q: How do I delete a question I created?**
A: Go to the question, tap the **...** menu, and select **Delete Question**. Note: Votes already cast are anonymized and retained for statistics.

### Questions & Voting

**Q: How many options can I add to a question?**
A: You can add 2-6 options. Most questions work best with 2-4 options.

**Q: Can I edit a question after posting?**
A: No, questions cannot be edited once posted to prevent manipulation. You can delete and repost if needed.

**Q: What if my question doesn't get any votes?**
A: Make sure your question is clear, relevant, and has an appropriate expiration time. Adding images helps attract more voters. Questions in popular topics tend to get more engagement.

**Q: Can I see who voted on my question?**
A: No, votes are anonymous. You can only see aggregated results and percentages.

**Q: Can I change my vote?**
A: No, votes are final once cast. This ensures authentic, thoughtful voting.

**Q: How many questions can I create per day?**
A: There's no hard limit, but you need points to create questions. Most users create 1-5 questions per day depending on their point balance.

### Points & Streaks

**Q: What happens if I lose my streak?**
A: You'll need to start over from day 1. Streaks are reset if you don't vote for 24 hours. Streak bonuses are lost until you rebuild the streak.

**Q: Can I buy points?**
A: Not currently. The only way to earn points is by participating in the community. We may introduce a point marketplace in the future.

**Q: Do points expire?**
A: No, points don't expire. Your balance carries over indefinitely.

**Q: What's the maximum number of points I can have?**
A: There's no maximum. You can accumulate as many points as you earn.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Who can see my votes?**
A: No one. Your individual votes are completely anonymous. Only aggregated results are shown.

**Q: Can I make my account private?**
A: Yes, go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy and enable private account mode. Only approved followers will see your content.

**Q: How do I report a bug or problem?**
A: Email us at support@besure.app with details about the issue, including screenshots if possible.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures. See our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
A: BeSure works on iOS 13+ and Android 8+ devices.

**Q: Do I need internet to use BeSure?**
A: Yes, an internet connection is required for voting and creating questions. Some content may be cached for offline viewing.

**Q: Why is the app slow?**
A: Try closing and reopening the app. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, contact support.

**Q: How do I update the app?**
A: Updates are available through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Enable auto-updates for the latest features.
