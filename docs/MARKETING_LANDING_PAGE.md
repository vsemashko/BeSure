## FAQ Section

### Is BeSure really free?
Yes! Core features are completely free forever. We'll introduce optional premium features in the future, but you'll never be forced to pay.

### How do I get points?
Vote on other people's questions. Every vote earns you +2 points. You can also earn bonuses through streaks, challenges, and achievements.

### Can I see who voted on my question?
No, all votes are anonymous. You only see aggregated results and percentages.

### Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your personal information. Read our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

### What kind of questions can I ask?
Anything appropriate! Fashion, shopping, travel, food, career, relationships, hobbies‚Äîif it's a decision, BeSure can help.

### Can I use BeSure without creating an account?
You can browse questions, but you need an account to vote and create questions.

### Is there a web version?
Currently, BeSure is mobile-only (iOS and Android). A web version is planned for the future.

### How do you prevent spam and fake accounts?
We use the point system, rate limiting, and AI detection to prevent abuse. Users can also report suspicious activity.
