## 3. How We Share Your Information

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### 3.1 Public Information
The following information is publicly visible by default:
- Username and display name
- Profile picture
- Bio/description
- Questions you create (unless set to private/friends-only)
- Your votes (anonymous by default)
- Points, streaks, and achievements
- Leaderboard rankings

You can control visibility of some information through privacy settings.

### 3.2 With Other Users
- Users who vote on your questions can see the question details
- Your followers can see your public questions
- Friends can see questions you set to "friends-only"
- Users can see aggregated, anonymized vote demographics

### 3.3 Service Providers
We share information with trusted third-party service providers:
- **Cloud Hosting:** Railway, Render (for backend services)
- **Database:** Supabase or similar PostgreSQL providers
- **Image Storage:** Cloudflare R2 or AWS S3
- **Caching:** Upstash Redis
- **Error Tracking:** Sentry
- **Push Notifications:** Expo Push Notification Service
- **Email Service:** SendGrid, Mailgun, or similar

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### 3.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Legal processes or government requests
- Protection of our rights and safety
- Investigation of fraud or security issues
- Enforcement of our Terms of Service

### 3.5 Business Transfers
If BeSure is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 3.6 With Your Consent
We may share your information for purposes not described in this policy with your explicit consent.

## 4. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### 4.1 Account Information
- **Access:** View your profile and account information in the app
- **Update:** Edit your username, display name, bio, and profile picture
- **Delete:** Request account deletion (see Section 4.6)

### 4.2 Content Control
- **Question Privacy:** Set questions to public, friends-only, or private
- **Anonymous Posting:** Hide your identity when creating questions
- **Vote Privacy:** Your individual votes are private by default
- **Delete Content:** Delete your questions (votes remain anonymized)

### 4.3 Communication Preferences
- **Push Notifications:** Manage in app settings or device settings
- **Email Notifications:** Unsubscribe via links in emails
- **Notification Types:** Control which notifications you receive

### 4.4 Data Access and Portability (GDPR Rights)
You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Export your data in a machine-readable format
- Receive information about how we process your data

To request your data, contact us at: privacy@besure.app

### 4.5 Data Correction and Deletion (GDPR Rights)
You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- Object to processing of your data
- Restrict processing in certain circumstances

### 4.6 Account Deletion
To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
2. Or email us at: support@besure.app

When you delete your account:
- Your profile and personal information are permanently deleted
- Your questions are anonymized or deleted (your choice)
- Your votes remain in aggregated form but are not attributed to you
- This action cannot be undone

### 4.7 Do Not Track
We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals as there is no industry standard for handling them.

### 4.8 Cookie Preferences
We use minimal cookies for essential functionality. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

- **Account Data:** Until you delete your account

- **Votes:** Anonymized data retained for analytics

- **Deleted Account Data:** Permanently deleted within 30 days
